Hi! My name is MacKenzie Harnett, and I am an incoming postdoctoral fellow at MIT. This website is primarily a means for me to document past work, provide commentary on that work, and to clarify my current goings-ons. 

I received my **B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Cornell University** in 2021, and I received my **Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Texas A&M University** in 2026. My recent work is within the haptics space, specifically with regards to how we interpret and generate tactile graphics across a range of different platforms and different tactile materials.  

Anyone interested in knowing more about my work or collaborating with me should reach out via my personal email (see ribbon at the bottom of the page).
